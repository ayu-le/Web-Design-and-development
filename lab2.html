Welcome! This page is about our home galaxy, the Milky Way. It is a barred spiral galaxy that contains our Solar System.

Key Facts About the Milky Way

Milky Way Statistics
Characteristic Value Unit
Type Barred Spiral Galaxy Classification
Estimated Age 13.6 Billion Years
Number of Stars 100 to 400 Billion

Major Components

  • Galactic Center: Home to a supermassive black hole.
  • Bulge: A tightly packed group of stars around the center.
  • Disk: Contains the spiral arms, where our Solar System resides.
  • Halo: A spherical component containing globular clusters.
